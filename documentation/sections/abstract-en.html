Abstract

AISHIP is a maritime ontology for the unified representation of vessel-related information. It extends the VesselAI ontology with additional vessel characteristics, enhanced trajectory data, detailed propulsion system modeling, and multimodal vessel representations such as images, sonar signatures, and textual descriptions. The ontology provides a standardized semantic model for integrating heterogeneous maritime data sources and supports consistent representation of vessels, their behavior, technical specifications, and operational context.
